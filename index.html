üîß Troubleshooting Guide

App Performance Issues:

  • App crashes or freezes: Force close the app and restart it. If issues persist, restart your device
  • Slow loading: Check your internet connection and ensure you have the latest app version
  • App won't open: Delete and reinstall the app from the App Store

Location & Prayer Times:

  • Incorrect prayer times: Verify location services are enabled and your location is accurate
  • Qibla direction seems wrong: Calibrate your device's compass by moving it in a figure-8 motion
  • Location not detected: Try toggling location services off and on in device settings

Notifications:

  • No prayer alerts: Check notification permissions and ensure sounds/vibrations are enabled
  • Delayed notifications: Ensure the app isn't in low power mode restrictions
